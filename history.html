History

Initiated under the leadership of Ven Piyadassi Thero in 1964.05.02. The first teacher is Mr. Piyadasa Algewaththa in this school. This school land was donat by Mr. W.A Piyadasa.

The first teacher was Mr. R.C Alagewaththa after being taken over by the government. It is said that the extent of the land at that time was one acre. Mr. Prisson Silva then became the Principal and at that time the school was conducted from grade 1 to grade 5. In 1983, Habaraduwa Member of Parliament GVS A new one storey 20 '* 80' building was handed over under the patronage of Mr.G.V.S. Silva.

Then the class size of the school increased to grade nine. Mr. Karunapala was Principal in 1990 and in the same year Mr. D.M.A. Sarathchandra was the principal for a few days. Subsequently Mr. Gunasekera was appointed as the Acting Principal. Prior to 1996.03.30, Lakshman was the Principal and the school office caught fire in March 1996, and past information could not be found. While the logbook exists from March 30 in 1996, 1997.06.23 Mr. M.K.S.G Wijerathna was appointed the principal post. After Mr. G.W Karunarathna has been appointed as the Principal in 2003. In 2012, the school was likely to close. At that time the school had two children and three teachers and on December 30, 2012, Mr. Sarath A Pathirana was appointed as the new Principal. Gradually, in 2017, the number of school children increased to 72, and teachers increased to 7. Out of the 15 students who applied for the 5 scholarship exams in 2017, three passed the 5 scholarship exams bar and the remaining twelve passed the 5 scholarship exams. In the year 2017, this school was one of the schools which obtained 100% results from the 5 scholarship exams.
